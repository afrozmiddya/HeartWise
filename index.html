Understanding Cardiovascular Health

Knowledge is the first step towards a healthy heart. Learn about heart disease, its risks, and how to prevent it.

üíô What is Heart Disease?

▼

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is a general term for conditions affecting the heart or blood vessels. It's usually associated with a build-up of fatty deposits inside the arteries (atherosclerosis) and an increased risk of blood clots. It can also be associated with damage to arteries in organs such as the brain, heart, kidneys and eyes. CVDs are the leading cause of death globally, taking an estimated 17.9 million lives each year.

üíì Common Risk Factors

▼

High blood pressure, smoking, high cholesterol, diabetes, sedentary lifestyle, and poor diet are common risk factors.

▼

Maintain a healthy diet, exercise regularly, quit smoking, manage stress, and get regular check-ups to prevent heart disease.
